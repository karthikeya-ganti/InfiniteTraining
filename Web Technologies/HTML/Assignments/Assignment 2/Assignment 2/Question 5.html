Hiphop Tamizha

Hiphop Tamizha is an Indian musical duo considered the pioneers of Tamil hip hop music, known for their albums, film compositions, and ventures into acting and direction.

Achievements:

  • Pioneered Tamil hip hop music in India.
  • Released India's first Tamil hip hop album, Hip Hop Tamizhan.
  • Successfully transitioned into film scoring, including Thani Oruvan.
  • Received prestigious awards like "Internet Sensation of the Year".
  • Championed Tamil language and culture through music.

Popular Releases:

  • Vaadi Pulla Vaadi
  • Meesaya Murukku
  • Takkaru Takkaru
